About Me

My name is Gerry Singh! My journey began in a close-knit community, where I spent my childhood in a small village surrounded by vast, open spaces. The deep connections with family and the freedom of the outdoors shaped much of who I am today, influencing both my path and perspectives, and guiding me at every step of my journey.


Now, I'm navigating life as a third-year Computer Science student at Seattle Pacific University, diving deep into the world of tech. I love collaborating with like-minded individuals to explore new ideas and tackle complex problems. My passion for technology goes beyond just the classroom; I’m always seeking ways to challenge myself and push the boundaries of what’s possible.


Outside of the classroom, you'll find me spending time with my cousins, hitting the gym, or diving into my favorite video games. Music is a huge part of my life, I listen daily and occasionally get creative, writing my own songs to express my thoughts.
