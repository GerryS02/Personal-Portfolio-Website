About Me

My name is Gerry Singh, and I was born and raised in the vibrant fields of Punjab, India, where I spent my childhood in a small village surrounded by vast, open spaces. The deep connections of family and the freedom of the outdoors shaped much of who I am today, influencing both my path and perspectives.


Now, I'm navigating life as a third-year Computer Science student at Seattle Pacific University, and I'm an active member of the CS club, where I connect with others who share my passion for tech.


Outside of the classroom, you'll find me spending time with my cousins, hitting the gym, or diving into my favorite video games. Music is a huge part of my life, I listen daily and occasionally get creative, writing my own songs to express my thoughts.
